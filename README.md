# odin-recipes
## Traditional Indian Sweets Recipes
This website is dedicated to sharing traditional Indian sweet recipes. Here, you'll find recipes for some of the most popular Indian sweets, including Gulab Jamun, Jalebi, and Laddu.

## Recipes
### Gulab Jamun
Gulab Jamun is a classic Indian sweet made with milk powder and soaked in a fragrant syrup. It's a popular dessert that's served at festivals, weddings, and other special occasions.

Click here to view the Gulab Jamun recipe.

### Jalebi
Jalebi is a crispy and juicy sweet that's popular all over India. It's made by deep-frying a batter made from all-purpose flour and then soaking it in a sweet syrup.

Click here to view the Jalebi recipe.

### Laddu
Laddu is a traditional Indian sweet that's made with various ingredients like flour, semolina, coconut, and nuts. They are round-shaped sweets that are often served at festive occasions like weddings and Diwali.
